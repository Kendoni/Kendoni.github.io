英语中考复习

学习地址

代词

（一）

We don’t know _ he is.

He is a doctor

A. what B. which C. who D. whom

A

不是所有的人都是who，what he is问他是什么工作

（二）

When shall we go to the museum, this afternoon or tomorrow morning?

_ is OK. I’m free these days.

A. all B. both C. Either D. Neither

C

A是全都，B是两者都，C是两者中一个，D是两者都不，还有none三者以上都不

只有两天中的一天出去，所以选C

（三）

Please give _ English book to her.

A. me B. I C. mine D. my

D

容易错选B，以为是give somebody something，其实是give something to somebody，我的英语书

（四）

_ are all college students, so we can help the old man solve the problem.

A. You, he and I B. He, you and I C. You, I and he D. I, he and you

A

你他我顺序，第二人称，第三人称，最后第一人称

（五）

Could I talk to you for _ minutes, Mike？

Sorry, I have _ time.

A. a few;little B. little;few C. few;little D. a little;a few

肯定 否定
后接可数名词 a few few
后接不可数名词 a little little

A

minutes可数肯定，time不可数否定

（六）

Who is singing in the classroom?

_ must be my sister. She likes singing.

A. It B. She C. This D. He

A

猜测时不能用明确的词，要用it代替所问的人

（七）

These sweaters are too small for me. Please show me _ one.

A. other B. others C. the others D. another

D

单词 用法
other 形容词，其他的
the other other作代词，one…the other…，两者中另一个
the others 特指剩下的所有，所有其他的
others 泛指其他，例如其他人
another 代词，另外一个（多个）/形容词，另一个
each other 我们（暗示两人）
one another 我们（暗示多人）

冠词（a/an/the/~）

a useful book | a university

an “F” | an hour

（一）

Lily’s mother is _ teacher in a school.

A. a B. an C. the D. /

A

后面是a school，不是特指，用a

（二）

My best friend Neil is _ honest boy. You can believe him.

A. a B. an C. the D. /

B

类似hour，honest中h不发音，an honest boy

（三）

Could you please help me to play _ guitar

A. a B. the C. an D. /

B

西洋乐器前要加the，球类运动前不加冠词

（四）

We should plant more trees in _ spring and we often plant trees on _ Sunday.

A. a; / B. /; the C. /; / D. the; a

C

四季，星期，月份，节假日前一般不加冠词

（五）

_ old man in _ brown coat over there is Mr. Jackson.

A. An, a B. An, the C. The, a D. The, the

C

前面特指那个老人，后面不是特指

（六）

一What _ cold weather.

Yes. But it’s _ unusual experience for us, we normally live in a hot place.

A. /; a B. a; an C. /; an D. a; the

C

weather不可数名词，unusual是元音开头

数词

序数词：

基数词 序数词
二十 twenty 第二十 twentieth
二十一 twenty-one 第二十一 twenty-first

分数：分子大于1，分母用复数

E.G. one third | two thirds | three and one fourth

倍数：2倍double，3或3倍以上的倍数用基数词+times

（一）

Nearly _ of the earth _ covered by sea.

A. three fourth, is B. three fourths, is C. three fourth, are D.three fourths, are

B

（二）

Which class won the match in the end?

I’m not quite sure. Perhaps _ did.

A. Class Third B. Third Class C. Class Three D. Three Class

C

E.G. Class Three, Grade One, Room 202

注意Class 在 Grade 前面

（三）

My brother is a _ boy and he can’t go to school now.

A. four years B. four-year-old C. four-years-old D. four years old

B

基数词-单数名词-adj.

（四）

What’s the date today?

It is _.

A. March the eighth B. March eight C. eight March D. eighth March

A

Match the eighth / March 8th

（五）

Where does your English teacher live?

He lives on _ floor of that red building.

A. five B. fifth C. the fifth D. the five

C

the + 序数词 + floor

（六）

There are _ days in a year.

A. three hundreds sixty-five

B. three hundred and sixty-five

C. three hundred and sixty five

D. three hundreds and sixty five

B

基数词 + hundred + and + 十位数-个位数（是-不是减号XD）
